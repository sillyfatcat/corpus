Privacy

Corpus Privacy Policy

This draft policy is suitable for a simple App Store support site and can be refined once you confirm the final data behavior of the app.

Overview

Corpus is designed to help users log workouts, review progress, and manage training data. We aim to collect as little personal information as possible.

Information You Provide

Corpus may store workout entries, routines, progress history, body metrics, and related training notes that you choose to enter into the app.

Health Data

If you enable HealthKit integration, Corpus may read or write workout-related information only with your permission and only for features you choose to use.

Cloud Sync

If cloud sync is enabled, your app data may be stored using Apple-provided cloud services associated with your account so your information can stay in sync across devices.

Support

If you contact support, your email address and the contents of your message will be used only to respond to your request.

Contact

For privacy questions or support requests, contact sshum@stupidfatcat.com.
